Campus Life

School Cafeteria

A nice place for lunch, EC, and special events!

This is my favorite place to go on a school day! The cafeteria serves hot lunch to those who don't bring lunch from home. We also have special events, like spring Program, Winter Sing, Math Contest, and more there!

School Auditorium

Year round, the auditorium is full of people!

The auditorium is the best place to have assemblies, programs, contests, and more! I have seen math, spelling, geography, and many more contests occur there, and we have merit assemblies, Memorial day assemblies, and more!

school achievements

8th grade Basketball championship
8th graders compete on the last day of chool to see who's class is best at sports.

On the last day of school, 8th graders round up on the basketball field with all other grades standing around watching the final championship for school basketball. It is a great entertainment and we see great players on the field.

Speech and Debate After-School classes
Kids learn to debate on simple topics and learn fundamentals of speech

After school, kids go to classes they can sign up for. Debate is one, where kids learn to debate on simple topics and learn fundamentals of speech.

DAR 5th - 8th grade competition
A history essay that can win them a fortune

From 5th grade to 8th grade, students have a history essay to write on a topic, where they imagine they are alive during that event. they write a story about it and have it sent to the principal, then to the chapter level, then a few more levels. It is very exciting and in 5th grade, i won the chapter!
